# IPL 2022 Analysis Summary

## Executive Takeaways

- Gujarat finished as the most efficient side in this dataset with 12 wins in 16 matches (75.0% win rate).
- Teams that won the toss also won the match 48.6% of the time, showing a meaningful but not decisive toss edge.
- Chasing teams won 50.0% of matches, which supports the field-first bias visible across the season.
- Kuldeep Yadav stood out as the strongest all-around impact player in this dataset with 4 player-of-the-match awards.
- Eden Gardens, Kolkata was the highest-scoring venue with an average total of 389.5 runs per match.

## Notable Match Extremes

- Biggest win by runs: Chennai by 91 runs.
- Biggest win by wickets: Delhi by 9 wickets.

## Portfolio Angle

This project turns a raw sports dataset into a reproducible analytics workflow by combining feature engineering, KPI extraction, automated reporting, and export-ready visualizations. It is designed to demonstrate data storytelling, Python-based analysis, and stakeholder-friendly communication.
